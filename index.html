About Valerie

I am a high school Library Media Specialist in Maine with a passion for making library education accessible to both staff and students. My teaching philosophy is the whole student approach: recognizing that students do not only exist in the classroom. I strengthen this teaching philosophy with training in the Lynn Lyon’s approach to anxiety and procrastination. Approaching digital literacy lessons from the whole student approach allows students to better access library lessons.
