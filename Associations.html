Associations

Within my career as a Library Media Specialist I plan to be part of these associations at one time or another.

  • Maine Library Association

    “The mission of the Maine Library Association is to promote and enhance the value of Maine libraries and librarianship, to foster cooperation among those who work in and for Maine libraries, and to provide leadership in ensuring that information is accessible to all citizens from their libraries” (Maine Library Association - Our Mission, n.d.).

  • Maine Association of School Libraries

    “The mission of the Maine Association of School Libraries is to provide connections for creating and advancing effective school library programs through professional development, advocacy, and community-building” (Maine Association of School Libraries - Home, n.d.).

  • New England Library Association

    “New England Library Association is the premier source for the creation and enrichment of diverse, dynamic New England library professional networks through conferences, scholarships, leadership development, advocacy, and collaboration with affiliated associations” (Bishop, n.d.).

  • School Library Journal

    “School Library Journal is the premiere publication for librarians and information specialists who work with children and teens” (About Us, n.d.).
